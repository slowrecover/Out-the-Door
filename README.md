给"想出门，但身体像灌了铅"的时候。它先帮你分清楚"为什么动不了" —— 没电、焦虑，还是低落 —— 因为每一种需要不同的第一步。然后把"出门"拆到再糟的日子也能做的最小动作，加一道三次呼吸的安抚门，再倒数把你推出门（腿先走，脑子后到）。出门前后各记一次心情，让你亲眼看见出门真的让自己好了一点。基于行为激活疗法 —— 行动先于动机，而不是反过来。
For when you want to go out but your body feels like lead. Out the Door first helps you name why you're stuck — no battery, anxiety, or low mood — since each needs a different first step. Then it shrinks "going out" down to something you can do on your worst day, adds a three-breath calming gate, and counts you out the door (legs first, brain later). You rate your mood before and after, so you can see that leaving actually helped. Built on Behavioral Activation — the evidence-based principle that action comes before motivation, not the other way around.
